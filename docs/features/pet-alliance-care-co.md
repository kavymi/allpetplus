# Care Services CO - Day Care, Boarding & Transport

**Division:** 10 of 12  
**Legal Name:** AP Plus Care, Inc.  
**Primary Focus:** Pet care services including daycare, boarding, and transportation  
**Business Model:** Service bookings + facility operations + transport network

---

## Executive Summary

The Care Services CO provides comprehensive day-to-day pet care through a network of facilities and service providers. From doggy daycare and boarding to pet taxi services, this division ensures pets are well-cared for when owners can't be present, creating peace of mind and recurring revenue.

**Mission:** "Care you can trust, when you can't be there"

## Cost Structure

**Facility Operations:**
- Rent/mortgage: 15-20% of revenue
- Labor: 40-50% of revenue
- Utilities: 5-8% of revenue
- Insurance: 3-5% of revenue
- Supplies: 3-5% of revenue
- Maintenance: 2-4% of revenue

**Transportation:**
- Vehicle costs: 20-25% of revenue
- Fuel: 10-15% of revenue
- Insurance: 8-12% of revenue
- Maintenance: 5-8% of revenue
- Driver labor: 30-35% of revenue

**Target Gross Margin:** 30-40%  
**Target EBITDA Margin:** 15-20% (at scale)

## Competitive Analysis

### Direct Competitors

**Rover/Wag (Marketplace):**
- Strengths: Large network, convenient booking
- Weaknesses: Variable quality, no facilities, insurance concerns

**Local Day Care/Boarding Facilities:**
- Strengths: Personal service, community presence
- Weaknesses: Limited capacity, inconsistent quality, basic technology

**National Chains (Camp Bow Wow, Dogtopia):**
- Strengths: Brand recognition, standardized experience
- Weaknesses: Corporate feel, limited services, premium pricing

### Our Competitive Advantages

1. **Integrated Ecosystem:** Pet data from entire alliance
2. **Technology Platform:** Easy booking, webcams, tracking
3. **Comprehensive Services:** Day care, boarding, transport, walking
4. **Quality Standards:** Certified facilities and staff
5. **Alliance Benefits:** Discounts and bundled services
6. **Real Estate Division:** Ownership of facilities (lower costs)
