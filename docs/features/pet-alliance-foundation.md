# Foundation - Philanthropic Programs

**Division:** 11 of 12  
**Legal Name:** AP Plus Philanthropic, Inc.  
**Entity Type:** 501(c)(3) Non-Profit Organization  
**Primary Focus:** Animal welfare, education, and community support  
**Business Model:** Grants + donations + corporate giving + fundraising events

---

## Executive Summary

The Foundation serves as the philanthropic arm of the Pet Solutions Alliance, dedicated to improving animal welfare, increasing pet adoption, supporting responsible pet ownership education, and reducing euthanasia rates. Through grants, community programs, and partnerships, this division creates positive social impact while strengthening the alliance's mission and brand reputation.

**Mission:** "Every pet deserves a loving home and a healthy, happy life"
