# Pet Food CO - Nutrition & Supplements

**Division:** 7 of 12  
**Legal Name:** AP Plus Foods, Inc.  
**Primary Focus:** Custom meal plans, nutrition, and supplements powered by AI  
**Business Model:** Subscription meal delivery + retail products + supplements

---

## Executive Summary

The Pet Food CO provides personalized, high-quality nutrition solutions for pets through custom meal plans, premium food products, and targeted supplements. By leveraging AI and health data from across the Pet Solutions Alliance ecosystem, this division ensures every pet receives optimal nutrition tailored to their unique needs.

**Mission:** "Precision nutrition for every pet's perfect health"

## Supplements & Health Products

### AI-Powered Pet Health Scorecard

**How It Works:**
1. Input pet profile (age, breed, weight, activity)
2. Upload health records (from Licensure/Healthcare CO)
3. Connect wearable data (optional)
4. AI analyzes and generates health score (1-100)
5. Personalized supplement recommendations

**Health Dimensions:**
- Joint health
- Digestive health
- Skin & coat
- Heart health
- Immune system
- Dental health
- Cognitive function
- Weight management

**Frequency:** Monthly updates, quarterly reassessment

## Cost Structure

### Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)

**Food Production:**
- Ingredients: 35-40% of revenue
- Manufacturing: 10-12% of revenue
- Packaging: 5-7% of revenue
- **Total COGS: 50-60%**

**Gross Margin Target:** 40-50%

### Shipping & Fulfillment

**Costs:**
- Shipping: $5-12 per box
- Cold chain (fresh/frozen): +$3-5
- Packaging materials: $2-4 per box

**Strategy:**
- Free shipping threshold: $35+
- 3PL fulfillment centers (5 locations nationwide)
- Optimized routing
- Subscription shipping: $8-10/month averaged

### Operating Expenses

**Marketing:**
- Customer acquisition: 20-25% of revenue (Year 1)
- Scaling down to 12-15% (Year 3)
- Target CAC: $80-150
- Target LTV: $1,200-2,000

**Technology:**
- AI/ML development: $2M/year

- Wearable integrations: $500K/year

**Operations:**
- Customer support: 3-4% of revenue
- Quality assurance: 1-2% of revenue
- Nutritionist team: $1.5M/year

**Target EBITDA Margin:** 15-20% (at scale)
