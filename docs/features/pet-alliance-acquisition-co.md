# Acquisition CO - Medical & Wellness Services

**Division:** 1 of 12  
**Legal Name:** Newburgh & Wellness Services, Inc. (For Humans)  
**Primary Focus:** Human medical and wellness services for pet caregivers  
**Business Model:** MSO (Management Services Organization) with state-specific entities

---

## Executive Summary

The Acquisition CO provides comprehensive medical and wellness services specifically designed for pet owners and caregivers. Recognizing that healthy, supported caregivers lead to healthier, happier pets, this division offers flexible healthcare solutions including telemedicine, behavioral health support, prescriptions, and specialty services.

## Risk Analysis

### Clinical Risks

**Medical Malpractice:**
- Mitigation: Comprehensive malpractice insurance, clinical protocols, provider credentialing

**Quality of Care:**
- Mitigation: Peer review, outcome tracking, continuous training, NCQA accreditation pursuit

**Patient Safety:**
- Mitigation: Clinical decision support systems, medication interaction checking, patient education

### Regulatory Risks

**Compliance:**
- Risk: HIPAA violations, licensing issues, fraud and abuse
- Mitigation: Dedicated compliance officer, regular audits, legal counsel, training programs

**Telehealth Regulations:**
- Risk: Varying state requirements, interstate practice limitations
- Mitigation: State-by-state compliance review, partnership with telehealth legal experts

### Operational Risks

**Technology:**
- Risk: Platform downtime, data breaches, integration failures
- Mitigation: Redundant systems, cybersecurity measures, disaster recovery plan, regular testing

**Staffing:**
- Risk: Provider shortages, high turnover, credentialing delays
- Mitigation: Competitive compensation, flexible schedules, strong culture, streamlined onboarding

### Financial Risks

**Reimbursement:**
- Risk: Insurance denials, regulatory changes affecting telehealth reimbursement
- Mitigation: Diversified revenue (subscriptions + fee-for-service), claims management expertise

**Market Adoption:**
- Risk: Slow uptake, high CAC, customer skepticism
- Mitigation: Strong alliance integration, clear value proposition, competitive pricing
