# Training CO - Pet & Human Education

**Division:** 6 of 12  
**Legal Name:** AP Plus Training, Inc.  
**Primary Focus:** Professional pet training and handler education programs  
**Business Model:** Training services + Certification programs + Partnerships

---

## Executive Summary

The Training CO provides comprehensive training services for pets and education programs for their owners through a nationwide network of certified trainers. By offering in-person classes, online courses, and certification programs, this division ensures well-trained pets and knowledgeable owners across the Pet Solutions Alliance ecosystem.

**Mission:** "Building better bonds through education and training"

## Competitive Analysis

### Direct Competitors

**PetSmart/Petco Training:**
- Strengths: Convenient locations, affordable
- Weaknesses: Variable trainer quality, basic curriculum, retail-focused

**Independent Trainers:**
- Strengths: Personalized service, flexible
- Weaknesses: Limited availability, no platform, inconsistent quality

**Online Training (Udemy, YouTube):**
- Strengths: Low cost, convenient
- Weaknesses: No personal support, lacks structure, variable quality

### Our Competitive Advantages

1. **Certified Trainer Network:** Consistent quality nationwide
2. **Integrated Ecosystem:** Training tied to pet profile and health data
3. **Flexible Options:** In-person, virtual, self-paced
4. **Certifications:** Recognized credentials (service dog, therapy dog)
5. **Technology Platform:** Easy booking, progress tracking
6. **Alliance Benefits:** Bundled discounts, cross-promotions
