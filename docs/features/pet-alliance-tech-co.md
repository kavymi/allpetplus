# AI Plus Tech CO - Technology & Innovation

**Division:** 8 of 12  
**Legal Name:** AP Plus Tech, Inc.  
**Primary Focus:** DNA testing, AI/ML infrastructure, and pet technology innovation  
**Business Model:** DNA testing kits + data licensing + AI platform services

---

## Executive Summary

The AI Plus Tech CO serves as the innovation engine of the Pet Solutions Alliance, providing cutting-edge technology including consumer DNA testing, health prediction models, AR/VR training tools, and the underlying AI infrastructure that powers personalized experiences across all divisions. This division transforms pet data into actionable insights while creating new revenue streams through testing products and B2B services.

**Mission:** "Technology that understands every pet's unique biology and behavior"
