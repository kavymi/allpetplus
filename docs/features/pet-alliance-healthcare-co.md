# Healthcare CO - Pet Insurance & Wellness

**Division:** 5 of 12  
**Legal Name:** AP Plus Insurance Co  
**Primary Focus:** Comprehensive pet insurance and preventative wellness plans  
**Business Model:** Insurance premiums + wellness subscriptions

---

## Executive Summary

The Healthcare CO provides comprehensive pet insurance coverage and preventative wellness plans designed to make pet healthcare accessible and affordable. By integrating with the Pet Solutions Alliance ecosystem, this division offers personalized coverage, seamless claims processing, and proactive health management.

**Mission:** "Every pet deserves access to quality healthcare"

## Coverage Details

### What's Covered

**Illnesses:**
- Cancer (all types)
- Diabetes
- Heart disease
- Kidney disease
- Allergies
- Arthritis
- Infections
- Digestive issues
- Skin conditions
- Respiratory conditions

**Accidents:**
- Broken bones
- Lacerations
- Poisoning
- Foreign body ingestion
- Burns
- Bite wounds
- Hit by car
- Torn ligaments

**Hereditary & Congenital (Silver+):**
- Hip dysplasia
- Heart defects
- Eye conditions
- Luxating patella
- Breed-specific conditions

**Chronic Conditions (All Plans):**
- Ongoing treatment covered
- No per-condition limits
- Lifetime coverage
- Continuous enrollment required

### What's Not Covered (Standard Exclusions)

**Pre-Existing Conditions:**
- Conditions before policy start
- Can be reviewed after 6-12 months symptom-free (curable conditions)

**Cosmetic Procedures:**
- Tail docking
- Ear cropping
- Dewclaw removal (non-medical)

**Breeding & Pregnancy:**
- Breeding costs
- Pregnancy complications
- C-sections (unless emergency)

**Behavioral Issues:**
- Training
- Behavioral modification
- Anxiety treatment (medication covered on some plans)

**Policy Options:**
- Coverage level (Bronze to Platinum)

- Reimbursement percentage
