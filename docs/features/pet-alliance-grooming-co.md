# Grooming CO - Salon Services & Products

**Division:** 9 of 12  
**Legal Name:** AP Plus Grooming, Inc.  
**Primary Focus:** Professional grooming services and premium grooming products  
**Business Model:** Service bookings + subscription plans + retail products

---

## Executive Summary

The Grooming CO provides professional grooming services through a network of certified groomers and flagship salons, complemented by a line of premium grooming products. By offering flexible booking, subscription plans, and mobile services, this division ensures pets look and feel their best while creating recurring revenue streams.

**Mission:** "Professional care for every coat, every day"

## Cost Structure

**Salon Operations:**
- Rent and utilities: 10-15% of revenue
- Labor (groomers): 35-45% of revenue
- Supplies: 5-8% of revenue
- Equipment: 3-5% of revenue

**Product COGS:**
- Manufacturing: 30-40% of retail price
- Packaging: 5-10%
- **Gross margin: 50-65%**

**Platform Costs:**
- Technology: $1M/year
- Marketing: 15-20% of revenue
- Customer support: 3-5% of revenue

**Target Gross Margin:** 55-65%  
**Target EBITDA Margin:** 18-22% (at scale)

## Competitive Analysis

### Direct Competitors

**PetSmart/Petco:**
- Strengths: Convenient, affordable, nationwide
- Weaknesses: Variable quality, corporate feel, limited appointments

**Independent Groomers:**
- Strengths: Personal service, expertise
- Weaknesses: Limited hours, inconsistent, hard to book

**Mobile Grooming:**
- Strengths: Convenient, stress-free
- Weaknesses: Limited service area, higher prices

### Our Competitive Advantages

1. **Integrated Ecosystem:** Grooming data enhances pet profiles
2. **Quality Network:** Certified groomers, consistent standards
3. **Technology Platform:** Easy booking, tracking, payments
4. **Subscription Model:** Predictable revenue, customer retention
5. **Premium Products:** Salon-quality, available retail
