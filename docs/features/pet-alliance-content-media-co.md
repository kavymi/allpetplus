# Content & Media CO - Entertainment & Marketing Platform

**Division:** 3 of 12  
**Legal Name:** AP Plus Media, Inc.  
**Primary Focus:** Pet entertainment, storytelling, and influencer marketing  
**Business Model:** Advertising + Sponsorships + Subscription content

---

## Executive Summary

The Content & Media CO creates, curates, and distributes engaging pet-focused content across multiple platforms. By building a community of pet lovers through entertainment, education, and competitions, this division drives brand awareness, engagement, and revenue across the entire Pet Solutions Alliance ecosystem.

**Mission:** "Entertain, educate, and connect the global pet community"

- Multiple pets, specific breed focus
