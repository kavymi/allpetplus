# Products CO - E-commerce & Custom Pet Gear

**Division:** 4 of 12  
**Legal Name:** AP Plus Products, Inc.  
**Primary Focus:** E-commerce platform for custom pet gear and lifestyle products  
**Business Model:** Direct-to-consumer e-commerce + customization tools

---

## Executive Summary

The Products CO operates a comprehensive e-commerce platform specializing in customizable, high-quality pet gear and lifestyle products. By offering both ready-made and custom-built products with an emphasis on personalization and quality, this division serves as a primary revenue driver while showcasing the alliance's commitment to individuality and style.

**Mission:** "Every pet is unique. Their gear should be too."

## Revenue Model

### Direct Sales

**Product Mix:**
- Ready-made products: 60% of orders
- Custom products: 35% of orders
- Matching sets: 5% of orders

**Average Order Value (AOV):**
- Ready-made: $45
- Custom: $120
- Matching sets: $180
- Overall AOV target: $75

**Gross Margins:**
- Ready-made: 50-60%
- Custom products: 60-70% (higher pricing)
- Matching sets: 55-65%

### Subscription Boxes

**Monthly Subscription:**
- "The Essential Box" - $39.99/month
  - 1 accessory
  - 2-3 toys
  - 1 treat bag
  - Surprise item
  
- "The Deluxe Box" - $79.99/month
  - 1 gear item (collar, leash, harness)
  - 3-5 toys
  - Grooming product
  - Treat assortment
  - Exclusive item

**Customization:**
- Size preference
- Breed-specific items
- Toy type preference (plush, rope, puzzle)
- Color/style preferences

**Retention:**
- Target: 70%+ retention rate
- Incentive: 10% discount for 6-month prepay

## Supply Chain & Manufacturing

### Manufacturing Partners

**Domestic Production (USA):**
- Custom/made-to-order items
- Quick turnaround (3-5 days)
- Higher quality control
- Premium pricing justified
- 40% of products

**International Production (Mexico, Vietnam):**
- Ready-made inventory
- Lower costs
- Bulk production
- 60% of products

**Quality Standards:**
- Material testing
- Safety certifications
- Durability testing
- Supplier audits

### Inventory Management

**Ready-Made Products:**
- Just-in-time inventory
- Safety stock: 30-day supply
- Seasonal adjustments
- Demand forecasting with AI

**Made-to-Order:**
- No inventory needed
- 5-7 day production time
- Dropship from manufacturing partner
- Higher margins

**Fulfillment:**
- 3PL partnership (ShipBob, Fulfillment by Amazon)
- 2-day shipping (standard)
- Next-day available (premium)
- International shipping (15+ countries)

### Cost Structure

**Cost of Goods Sold (COGS):**
- Products: 35-45% of revenue
- Shipping: 8-10% of revenue
- Packaging: 3-5% of revenue
- **Target gross margin: 45-55%**

**Operating Expenses:**
- Marketing: 20-25% of revenue (Year 1), scaling down to 15-18% (Year 3)
- Technology (platform, builder): $500K/year
- Customer service: 3-5% of revenue
- Operations & admin: 5-8% of revenue

**Target EBITDA Margin:** 15-20% (at scale)
